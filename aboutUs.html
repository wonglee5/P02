Pet Gentle Cafe

Welcome to Pet Gentle Cafe! We are a pet-friendly cafe for pet lover and pet owners who want to enjoy a nice cup of tea, coffee or baked goods. Our gentle cafe pets with chic fashion will greet you at the doorstep. Come and enjoy relaxing day with lovely and fluffy companions.

Also, feel safe to drop your pets here while you are away. We have specialists to take care of your love and make sure they are happy as at home. Don`t worry about it! We will also take of your loves when you are on a bussiness trip or a vacation even if the cafe is not open.
