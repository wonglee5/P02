User-flow & Wireframes

There are five important user-flows for Pet Gentle Cafe:

  1. About Us: looking for general information (cafe location, hours, email for reservation, FAQ).
  2. Reservation: making a reservation to secure a spot in the cafe.
  3. Our Dogs: information about personality, story and introduction of dogs of our cafe and surprise visits from local social media famous dogs.
  4. Menu: our price for food and services.
  5. Gift/Shop: our purchase goods for dogs and customers.
