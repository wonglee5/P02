Welcome to Dogentle Cafe!

Dogentle Cafe is a dog-friendly cafe for pet lovers and pet owners who want to enjoy a nice cup of tea, coffee or baked goods. Our cute cafe dogs can be a playmate with you or for your dogs! Come and enjoy fun and amusing day with cute furry friends.

Dogentle Cafe aims to become a cozy space where dog lovers can connect with local dog communities. Enjoy our dog of the day gallery starring many photographs of social media famous dogs.

Dogentle Cafe is a elegant yet hipster style cafe and dog-friendly space for pet lovers and pet owners who want to enjoy a cup of aromatic tea, coffee or delicious baked goods. Our cute cafe dogs can be a companion for your afternoon tea time. Come and visit our gentlemanly dogs with a cute bowtie.

Dogentle Cafe aims to become a warm and cozy space where dog lovers can connect with local dog communities. Enjoy our vintage style cafe and dog of the day gallery starring many photographs of social media famous dogs.
